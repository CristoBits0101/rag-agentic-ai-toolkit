Company Policy Handbook

Email Policy
Company email is for business communication only. Employees must not send customer records through personal mail accounts. Sensitive attachments must be encrypted before sending.

Smoking Policy
Smoking and vaping are prohibited inside offices, meeting rooms, and within ten meters of entrances. Employees may use only the designated outdoor smoking area during approved breaks.

Remote Work Policy
Hybrid employees may work from home two days each week with manager approval. Staff must remain reachable in the company chat during core hours.

Security Policy
Multi factor authentication is required for all internal systems. Laptops must auto lock after five minutes and confidential data must be stored only in approved tools.
